Mirage

Holographic Trickster

Mirage is the kind of guy who likes to stand out. The youngest of four brothers, he perfected
the art of fooling around to get attention. The one thing he took seriously was Holo-Pilot technology:
introduced to the illusion-creating tech by his engineer mother, he poured over the mechanisms and
learned all he could about them. Even when his brothers went MIA during the Frontier War, Mirage and his
mother continued to develop holo devices, and the work brought them closer.

While working as a bartender to make ends meet, Mirage heard amazing stories from his patrons about
the Apex Games and the wealth and glory that came with victory. As good as both of those sounded, he knew
he couldn’t risk leaving his mother childless – until she gave him a set of customized holo devices
and told him to follow his dream. Mirage is now the life of the Apex Games, outwitting opponents and charming
audiences across the Outlands.

Real Name - Elliott Witt
Age - 30
Home Planet - Solace
Tactical Ability - Psyche Out
Passive Ability - Now You See Me...
Ultimate Ability - Life of the Party
